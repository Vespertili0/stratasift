# Title: Discovery of a 40% lower energy barrier for the rate-limiting step

## Core Insight
> By performing exhaustive permutations of like atoms during NEB calculations using the MLFF, the authors identified an optimized reaction path for the rate-limiting step (HCOO + H → H2COO). This path, characterized by an oxygen atom occupying the oxygen vacancy, reduces the energy barrier to 1.0 eV, representing a 40% reduction over previously known paths.
